Oklahoma Sooners Football

One of the Most Historic Programs in College Football

Stationed in Norman, Oklahoma, the Sooners have been lacing up the football cleats since 1895 (12 years before Oklahoma was declared a state). Since then, the program has provided some of the most talented athletes the sport has ever seen.

162 Sooners have been named first-team All-American throughout their history. These legends include Adrian Peterson, Brian Bosworth, and Baker Mayfield.
